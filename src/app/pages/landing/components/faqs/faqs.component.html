Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is InternConnect?

    InternConnect is a dedicated platform for requesting endorsement letters and viewing accredited companies in UST-CICS. It is part of the major requirements in the Practicum course for CICS prior to starting one's internship.

  • What's an endorsement request for?

    It is a form that is processed by the Practicum Coordinator indicating the pertinent details about the company where a student intends to have the internship during the term. An endorsement letter will be generated based on the entries approved on the form. it will explicitly state the lines of work that an intern must work on during the internship.

  • What do I have to prepare prior to submitting an endorsement request?

    As a student, you will need the following:

    1. Company Acceptance Letter
    2. Company Profile
    3. Name, Email, and Designation of Contact Person

  • How do I check the progress of the endorsement?

    To visit the status page:

    1. Visit the landing page
    2. Click 'Status'

    The student will also receive email notifications regarding updates on the request.

  • What other requirements should I be aware of?

    Other gradeable components are not part of this platform. You may reach out to your Practicum Coordinator for the requirements to be submitted via UST Cloud Campus (Blackboard).

  • What should I do if the company I applied for/was accepted to is not accredited?

    You will need to prepare a Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) signed by the company and the CICS administrators. A template is available for you to download in the landing page.
