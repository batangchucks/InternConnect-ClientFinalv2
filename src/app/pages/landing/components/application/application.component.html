What can you do with InternConnect?

Here's what you can do as a student taking up the practicum course.

Search companies

The College has a number of accredited companies that you can use as a reference when applying. You can also visit their website by visiting their profile page.

Submit an endorsement request

Once you've settled your requirements for the endorsement on your end,
you can use our platform to submit an endorsement request letter.

The practicum coordinator will receive this and review your request.

Check your status

After submitting, you can track the progress of the request by visiting the status page.

You'll also receive e-mail notifications for every update on the request.
